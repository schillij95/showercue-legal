ShowerCue Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

This privacy policy explains what happens to your data when you use ShowerCue, including reminders, schedule settings, and local progress tracking.

Information We Collect

ShowerCue may collect the following categories of information:

  • App settings and shower history stored locally on your device.
  • Location data used on your device when you choose to use arrival-home reminders.
  • Analytics events about app usage, such as onboarding progress, reminder flows, shower starts and finishes, settings changes, and feature usage.
  • Crash and diagnostics information used to detect errors and improve app reliability.

How We Use Information

  • To run core app features such as schedules, reminders, and streaks.
  • To improve reminder reliability, feature quality, and app stability.
  • To understand which parts of the app are used and where users run into issues.

Location Data

If you enable the arrival-home reminder feature, ShowerCue uses location data to help detect when you arrive at your saved home location and to send the relevant reminder. This may require background location permission so the reminder can work when the app is closed.

ShowerCue uses location data for arrival-home reminders. Raw coordinates are not sent in analytics events.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

ShowerCue uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics in native builds. The app includes a Settings preference for product analytics. When that preference is off, ShowerCue turns off Firebase Analytics collection. When it is on, ShowerCue may send app-usage events and reminder diagnostics such as event names, app variant, reminder kind, trigger type, date keys, scheduled times, action names, counts, and product or entitlement identifiers when relevant. Crash reporting remains enabled so the app can detect failures and improve reliability, and Crashlytics may receive limited app metadata such as app variant and application identifier. ShowerCue does not use analytics for third-party advertising.

Data Sharing

ShowerCue may share limited data with service providers used to operate the app, such as Firebase for analytics and crash reporting. ShowerCue does not sell personal data.

Your Choices

  • You can turn off product analytics in the app settings.
  • You can disable notifications in the app or in system settings.
  • You can revoke location permissions in system settings.

Turning off product analytics does not disable crash reporting or other diagnostics needed to keep the app working.

Data Retention

Data stored locally by ShowerCue remains on your device until you remove it through the app, clear app data, or uninstall the app. Analytics and crash data may be retained by service providers according to their own retention policies.

Children

ShowerCue is not directed to children under 13.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact Gentle Signal support at: gentlesignal.studio@gmail.com. ShowerCue is operated from Switzerland.
